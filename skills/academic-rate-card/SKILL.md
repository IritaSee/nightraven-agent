# Academic Rate Card

Standardized fee structure for teaching, supervision, and academic consulting.

## When to Use
- When quoting fees for teaching sessions.
- When calculating honorariums for student supervision (thesis, projects).
- When planning budgets for workshops or courses.

## Rate Structure

### 1. Teaching Sessions
- **Standard Session (~2 hours)**: Rp 500,000
- **Range**: Rp 300,000 - Rp 750,000 (depending on complexity and preparation required).

### 2. Supervision Sessions
- **Standard Supervision**: Rp 125,000 - Rp 225,000 per session.
- **Complex/Thesis Supervision**: Rp 250,000 - Rp 500,000 per session.

### 3. Courses & Workshops
- **Full Workshop/Course**: Rp 1,500,000+ (depending on duration and materials).

## Usage Steps
1. Identify the type of academic engagement (Teaching, Supervision, or Workshop).
2. Determine the complexity and duration.
3. Select the appropriate rate from the card.
4. Communicate the quote to the institution or student.

## Example
**Scenario**: A 2-hour guest lecture on "Cognitive Psychology".
- **Category**: Teaching Session.
- **Rate**: Rp 500,000.
